# GHC plans for 8.2.2






This page is our road-map for what will be in 8.2.2.


## Current status



The release is currently stabilizing.


## Dates



8.2.2-rc1 was released on 1 October 2017.
8.2.2-rc2 will be released the week of 16 October 2017.
